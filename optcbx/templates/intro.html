What OPTCbx is?

This web application aims to showcase the potential of OPTC Box exporter (OPTCbx). Here you will find a short description of what OPTCbx is and also, you will be able to try it.

OPTCbx can analyze your character box screenshots, and obtain all the characters within them. OPTCbx does that without any manual intervention.

By its own, OPTCbx, does not have any utility apart from displaying your character box in a fancy manner.

I rather say that power (or utility) comes with contributions. I believe that OPTCbx can be a key point combined with amazing projects such as:

  • NakamaNetwork: With OPTCbx you are not far from automatically exporting your teams or your box to this extraordinary website.
